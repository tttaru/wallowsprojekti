WALLOWS

Music Members Tour Newsletter


Wallows is an alternative rock band based in Los Angeles. The band consists of three members; Dylan Minette, Braeden Lemasters and Cole Preston. The members have known each other since 2011 through a student program and they started releasing music as Wallows in 2017, which lead them to sign a record deal with Atlantic Records in 2018. Since then they have released post-punk/indie rock music, like two times platinum certified Are You Bored Yet? (featuring Clairo) and the latest full-length album Tell Me That It's Over.
